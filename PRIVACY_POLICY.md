# Privacy Policy for Moa

**Last updated: November 25, 2025**

## Introduction

Moa ("the App") is a Korean language learning application developed by wydry. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user data.

## Data Collection and Storage

### Local Data Storage (Current Version)
Moa currently stores all user data **locally on your device** using AsyncStorage. This includes:
- Flashcard decks and cards you create
- Study progress and statistics
- App settings and preferences
- Handwriting practice data

**In the current version (v1.0), we do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.**

### Future Features (Lessons & Community Library)
Future versions of Moa may include optional backend features:
- **Lessons System**: Access to curated learning modules (read-only, no personal data collected)
- **Community Library**: Optional ability to browse and download decks shared by others
- **Deck Sharing**: Optional ability to upload your own decks to share with the community

**These features will be:**
- Entirely optional (you can continue using the app offline)
- Announced with privacy policy updates before release
- Designed with minimal data collection (no user accounts required for basic usage)
- Available to a limited group initially (classmates and study partners)

If these features are implemented, we will update this privacy policy and notify users before collecting any data.

## Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

### Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)
- Used to send study reminders and notifications
- You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time

### Internet Access
- Used for downloading ML Kit language models (Korean/Japanese handwriting recognition)
- May be used in future versions to access optional lesson content or community library
- In the current version, no user data is transmitted over the internet

## Third-Party Services

### Google ML Kit Digital Ink Recognition
- Used for handwriting recognition on-device
- Language models (Korean/Japanese) are downloaded to your device
- Recognition processing occurs entirely on your device
- No handwriting data is sent to Google or any external service

### Expo Framework
- The App is built using Expo/React Native
- Expo may collect anonymous usage statistics (crash reports, performance metrics)
- No personally identifiable information is shared
- See Expo's privacy policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

## Data Sharing

### QR Code Sharing
- You can share flashcard decks with others via QR codes
- This is an optional feature and only shares the specific deck data you choose to export
- No personal information is included in shared decks

### No Third-Party Data Sharing
- We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not serve advertisements

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- Data security depends on your device's security measures
- We recommend using device encryption and screen lock features

## Data Retention and Deletion

- All data remains on your device until you choose to delete it
- You can delete individual cards, decks, or uninstall the app to remove all data
- Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all local data

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age. The App can be used by learners of all ages, with all data stored locally on the device.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or the App's data practices, please contact:

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You have complete control over your data
- You can export your data (CSV export feature)
- You can delete any or all data at any time
- You can uninstall the app to remove all data
